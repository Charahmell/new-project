How to make pancakes

Pancakes is one of the easiet breakfast options. It's a form of pastry that makes use of
ingredients that are easy to get in a household.

The following are ingredients used to make pancakes
(for one serving):

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • Butter

Steps to follow:

  1. Sift one cup of flour in a bowl
  2. This bowl contains only the dry ingredients only so in it you want to add a pinch of salt,
    three tbl of sugar and one tsp of baking powder
  3. In another bowl beat one egg, our in a cup of milk and combine
  4. Add a drop of vanilla extract (Optional)
  5. The next step is too gently pour the dry ingredients mis into the wet and fold till a smooth and thick consistency
  6. Preheat a nonstick pan with alittle butter and fry youtr batter on low heat in medium size (for aesthetics)
  7. When bubbles start poppingon your pancakes it's ready to flip
  8. flip and serve.
